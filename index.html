Welcome to Apples & Pears Gifts

Tel: 01932 342276
e-mail: applesandpearsgifts@outlook.com

Apples & Pears, (formally Heather Forsters) is a family run business that has been trading in West Byfleet for 50 years. The current owners John and Christine Morrison have owned the shop for last 10 years. We pride ourselves on customer service as well as trying to offer a unique way of shopping.

We are stockists of some of the very big brands in the gift industry such as; Lladro, Royal Crown Derby, Dartington Crystal, Portmerion Group and many more!
